About Our TB Detection Model

Tuberculosis (TB) is a highly contagious and life-threatening infectious disease caused by the bacteria Mycobacterium tuberculosis. It primarily affects the lungs but can also target other organs in the body. TB remains a significant global health concern, with millions of new cases and deaths reported each year, particularly in developing countries with limited healthcare resources.
